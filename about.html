About Us:


Welcome to Sanskriti, the flagship cultural extravaganza of Indian Institute of Technology! Our festival is a dynamic celebration of art, culture, and innovation, featuring a diverse array of events, from high-energy dance and music competitions to captivating theatrical performances and fashion shows.


This year, we’re bringing an incredible lineup of proshows, where renowned artists and performers will take the stage. Additionally, hands-on workshops offer students a chance to learn and grow in fields like photography, filmmaking, music, and more. It’s the perfect fusion of fun, creativity, and learning!


Whether you’re a participant or a spectator, [Festival Name] is the ultimate platform to express your talents, enjoy spectacular performances, and be part of an unforgettable cultural experience. Join us and make memories that will last a lifetime!
